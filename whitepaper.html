Orbix (ORX) Whitepaper

Introduction

Orbix (ORX) is a scalable, secure, and upgradeable ERC-20 token deployed on the Polygon network. It features a 2% transaction fee, automated fee distribution, and integrated staking capabilities.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 900,000,000 ORX
  • Transaction Fee: 2% (sent to 0x538f5aE9Ce9a68d3D49536cEDE650D0210C73DBf)
  • Upgradeable Contract: Enabled

Staking

Holders can stake ORX to earn fixed yields over time. More info will be available on the staking page.
